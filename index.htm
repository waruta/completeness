Parametric Lattice Generator

Parametric toolpaths for large-format pellet-based 3D printing

Overview

This open-source Grasshopper script enables the generation of continuous, user-defined toolpaths for lattice structures tailored to extrusion-based pellet printers. It empowers researchers, students, and artists to push the boundaries of scalable 3D printing without relying on SLA/SLS.
